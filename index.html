My Values and How I'll Nurture Them

Honesty and Transparency

Being honest allows others to be confident in what you tell them; building into transparency, which means you will discuss what you are thinking.
I will always be clear about what I'm doing.

Commitment and Persistence

Commitment ensures I will finish what I start, and persistence guarantees that obstacles will not stop me.
I will relentlessly pursue the object.

Optimism and Good Faith

Optimism fuels commitment and persistence. To proceed in good faith encourages taking the greatest of available actions.
I will always be positive.

Humility and Self-Awareness

To be humble means you will accept what is better, along with self-awareness to help you acknowledge and let go of what is worse.
I will readily accept criticism, and be self-critical.
